Resources

• Study Tips

• Know current examples. Human geography is a dynamic field that deals with contemporary issues and events. You should be able to provide relevant and specific examples to support your answers and arguments.

• Know the terminology and concepts. Human geography has a lot of terms and concepts that you need to memorize and understand. You should be able to define, explain, and apply these terms and concepts to different scenarios and questions.
