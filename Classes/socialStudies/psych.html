About The Course

In this class, you’ll explore ideas about behavioral and mental processes. You’ll learn different concepts of Psychology through reading and analyzing research data.

About The Exam

• Exam Date: Tuesday, May 2nd 12:00 PM

• Multiple Choice: 100 Questions, 1 hour and 10 minutes, 67% of the total score

• Free Response: 2 Questions, 50 minutes, 33% of total score

• Concept Application (7 pt)

• You’ll be presented with a scenario in which you’ll be asked to apply different concepts to show your understanding.

• Research Question (7 pt)

• You’ll be given some data from research and you’ll be asked to form some sort of research using the data given.
