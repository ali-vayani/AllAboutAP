About The Course

AP Microeconomics is an introductory college-level course in economics. Students gain a better understanding of the behavior of individuals within an economic system as a whole. The use of graphs, charts, and data to analyze and describe economic concepts will be present.

About The Exam

• Exam Date: Friday, May 5th 12:00 PM

• Multiple Choice: 60 Questions | 1 hour and 10 minutes | 66% of the total score

• Multiple Choice will include individual, single, and sets of questions

• The questions will be based on:

• Economic content knowledge and reasoning across course topics and skills in skill categories 1, 2, and 3

• Free Response: 3 Questions | 1 hour | 33% of the total score

• 1 Long FRQ (50% of section score)

• Total of 5 parts

• 2 Short FRQs (each are 25% of section score)

• Total of 4 parts

• Students will be asked to:

• Conduct numeral analysis

• Make predictions about several economic concepts

• Construct visual representations, such as graphs

• Explain economic concepts
