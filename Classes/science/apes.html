About The Exam

• Exam Date: Tuesday, May 2nd 8:00 AM

• Multiple Choice: 80 Questions | 1 hour 30 Minutes | 60% of Exam Score

• Individual questions

• Set-based questions

• 3-4 sets contain quantitative data

• 3-4 sets contain qualitative data

• 2 sets contain text-based sources

• Free Response: 3 Questions | 1 hour 10 Minutes | 40% of Exam Score

• Question 1: Asks students to design and analyze an investigation

• Question 2: Asks students to analyze an environmental problem and propose a solution using models and representations

• Question 3: Asks students to analyze an environmental problem and propose a solution using calculations
